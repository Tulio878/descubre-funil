Response Time

Our team responds to all inquiries within 24 to 48 business hours, Monday through Friday. Messages are answered in chronological order based on when they arrive. Emails received on weekends or holidays will be processed on the next business day.

What We Can Help With

  • Access issues or login problems
  • Password recovery
  • Questions about course content
  • Technical support related to the members area
  • Payment confirmation and billing questions
  • Refund requests according to our Refund Policy

What Is Not Included in Support

  • Personalized business consulting beyond what is taught in the course
  • WhatsApp monitoring, spying or any unauthorized data collection
  • Legal, financial or operational advice unrelated to the course materials

Important Notes

  • All communication must be done via our official support email or the members area.
  • We do not provide support via personal social media, messaging apps or private phone numbers.
  • Please include your full name, purchase email and order number when contacting support.
