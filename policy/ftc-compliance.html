FTC Compliance

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Transparency

FGA Global Media LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) is committed to operating in full compliance with the U.S. Federal Trade Commission (FTC) guidelines regarding advertising, endorsements, testimonials, and consumer protection. This page outlines how we comply with those standards across our website, marketing materials, and digital products.

2. Truth in Advertising

We strive to ensure that all claims made on our website, in our advertisements, and within our products are truthful, accurate, and supported by reasonable evidence. We do not make false or misleading statements about the features, benefits, or expected results of our products.

All product descriptions, pricing, and terms of purchase are presented clearly before checkout, in accordance with FTC requirements for transparent online commerce.

3. Endorsements and Testimonials

Any testimonials, reviews, or endorsements displayed on our website or within our marketing materials reflect the genuine opinions of the individuals providing them. We follow the FTC’s Guides Concerning the Use of Endorsements and Testimonials in Advertising, including:

  • Disclosing material connections between us and any endorser, when applicable.
  • Indicating clearly when results shown are not typical of what an average user can expect.
  • Avoiding the use of fabricated, scripted, or paid testimonials presented as authentic experiences.

4. Affiliate and Partner Relationships

From time to time, we may enter into affiliate or partner relationships with third-party companies whose products or services we recommend. When we do, we disclose those relationships clearly. We may receive compensation if you purchase a product or service through an affiliate link, but this never influences our commitment to recommending only what we believe provides genuine value to our customers.

5. Earnings and Results Claims

We do not guarantee any specific financial result, business outcome, or level of success from the use of our products. Any examples of earnings, income, sales, or business growth shared on this website are for illustrative purposes only and should not be interpreted as guarantees.

For full details on this topic, please review our Earnings Disclaimer.

6. Subscription Disclosures and Negative Option Marketing

For products sold on a recurring subscription basis, we comply with the FTC’s rules on negative option marketing. Specifically, we disclose:

  • The full price of each billing cycle and the frequency of billing.
  • The duration of any free trial, if applicable, and what happens at the end of that trial.
  • How customers can cancel their subscription at any time without penalty.

These terms are presented clearly on the checkout page before any payment is collected.

7. Data Privacy and Children’s Privacy

We respect the privacy of our customers and visitors. Our practices regarding the collection, use, and protection of personal data are described in our Privacy Policy. Our products and services are intended for adults aged 18 or older, and we do not knowingly collect personal information from children under the age of 13, in accordance with the Children’s Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA).

8. Refund and Cancellation Practices

We provide clear, accessible information about refunds and cancellations through our Refund Policy and Cancellation Policy. All customers have the right to request a refund within the timeframe specified in those policies, and we process those requests in accordance with FTC consumer protection standards.
