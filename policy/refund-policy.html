Explodely Refund Policy

Last updated: April 30, 2026

1. Our Commitment to Customer Satisfaction

FGA Global Media LLC (“we,” “us,” “our”) is committed to providing high-quality educational digital products. We understand that not every product will meet every customer’s expectations, and we have established this Refund Policy to ensure a fair and transparent refund process for all purchases made through our payment processor, Explodely.

2. 60-Day Money-Back Guarantee

We offer a 60-day money-back guarantee on all of our digital products. If you are not satisfied with your purchase, you may request a full refund within 60 days of the original purchase date.

This guarantee applies to:

  • One-time purchases of our digital courses and training programs.
  • The first billing cycle of any subscription product.

3. Subscription Cancellations and Recurring Charges

For subscription-based products, you may cancel your subscription at any time. Once cancelled, you will not be billed for any future billing cycles. Cancellation requests must be submitted before your next billing date to avoid being charged for the upcoming period.

If you have already been charged for a new billing cycle and cancel within the 60-day guarantee window of your original purchase, you may still be eligible for a refund of that charge in accordance with the terms of this policy.

4. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please contact our support team using one of the following methods:

  • Email: [email protected]
  • Support page: /policy/support.html
  • Explodely billing support: https://help.explodely.com/support/tickets/new

When requesting a refund, please include the following information so we can locate your transaction:

  • Full name used at the time of purchase.
  • Email address used at checkout.
  • Order or transaction number provided by Explodely.
  • Reason for your refund request (optional but appreciated, as it helps us improve).

5. Refund Processing Time

Once your refund request is approved, the refund will be processed within 5 to 10 business days. The funds will be returned to the original payment method used at the time of purchase. Depending on your bank or card issuer, it may take an additional few business days for the refund to appear in your account.

6. Exceptions and Limitations

Refunds may be denied in cases involving abuse of the guarantee, including but not limited to:

  • Repeated refund requests for the same product.
  • Refund requests submitted after the 60-day guarantee period.
  • Evidence of fraudulent activity or chargeback abuse.

7. Chargebacks

We kindly ask that you contact us directly before initiating a chargeback through your bank or credit card issuer. In most cases, we are able to resolve refund requests quickly and amicably. Initiating a chargeback without first attempting to resolve the matter with our support team may delay the resolution of your case.

8. Changes to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this Refund Policy at any time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated revision date. Your continued use of our products after such changes constitutes your acceptance of the revised policy.
